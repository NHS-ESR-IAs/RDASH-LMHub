Volunteer Learning Hub

Welcome to your dedicated training space. To ensure the safety and wellbeing of our patients and the RDaSH community, all volunteers are required to complete these 9 core standards.

Training Requirement 9 Mandatory Modules

Click the tiles below to access ESR and complete your specific updates.

Standard 1: Role & Responsibilities

Rights, raising concerns, and the importance of reporting errors (Once Only).
Standard 2: Communication

Data security, record keeping, and conflict management (Yearly).
Standard 3: Respect for Everyone

Equality, human rights, and challenging prejudice (3 Yearly).
Standard 4: Safeguarding

Safeguarding adults/children and the PREVENT strategy (3 Yearly).
Standard 5: MH, Dementia & LD

Improving interactions with individuals facing these challenges (Once Only).
Standard 6: Health & Safety

Hazard identification, risk assessment, and fire safety (2 Yearly).

Standard 7: Fluids & Nutrition

Food safety, hygiene, and recognizing poor hydration (Once Only).
Standard 8: Basic Life Support

Essential theory and training for Resuscitation (Once Only).
Standard 9: Moving & Assisting

Safe posture, injury risk factors, and back care (2 Yearly).

Technical & Account Support

I’m having technical problems. Who do I contact?

Contact the IT helpdesk using their online portal.

I forgot my ESR login, or ESR will not load. What do I do?

Contact the IT helpdesk using their portal.

Do I need to be logged in to ESR to join a course?

Yes. When you try to book a course, the system will ask you to log in.

Can I use the learning hub on my mobile?

Yes. You can use it if you have the link.

Finding & Managing Training

Where do I find my mandatory training (MAST)?

Search for MAST in the Course Prospectus (Learner Catalogue panel).

How do I find courses and join them?

Go to the Learner panel for help and the user guide.

Where can I see the courses I’m booked on, past courses, and my certificates?

You can see all of these in ESR, just like before.

How do I book myself onto face-to-face (non-MAST) training?

Go to the Learner panel for help and the user guide.

Can I watch a training session after it has finished?

If it was recorded, it will be in the Video Vault panel once uploaded.

Has my ESR changed because of the new learning hub?

No. Nothing has changed. The hub just helps you find the right ESR pages more quickly.

Educators, Managers & Special Roles

Go to the Educator panel for help and the user guide.

I want to run a training session. What do I do?

Go to the Educator panel for help and the user guide.

How do I book a training room?

Go to the Training Rooms panel for more information.

Do I need to use the volunteer panel?

No. Only volunteers need to use this panel.
